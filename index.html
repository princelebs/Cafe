Our Story

1952

The Beginning

Giuseppe's grandfather, Antonio Pereto, opens the first family coffee shop in Naples, Italy, establishing our coffee legacy with traditional roasting methods.

1
2

1985

Second Generation

Giuseppe's father, Marco, expands the business and develops the signature Pereto roasting technique that defines our unique flavor profile and aroma.

2018

New Horizons

Maria and Giuseppe Pereto bring the family tradition to America, opening Pereto Cafe with a vision to share authentic Italian coffee culture.
