Our Story

A journey of passion, quality, and community

The Beginning

Founded in 2008 by coffee enthusiast Marco Pereto, our cafe began as a small roastery in the heart of the city. Marco's vision was simple yet profound: to create a space where exceptional coffee meets genuine hospitality.

After traveling through coffee-growing regions of Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, Marco returned with not just beans, but stories, relationships, and a deep understanding of what makes truly exceptional coffee.

Direct trade relationships with farmers
Small-batch roasting techniques
Community-focused approach
